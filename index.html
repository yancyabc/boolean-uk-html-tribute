The 3 Longest Rivers of the World

Amazon River

  • 6,992km long
  • Originates in the Andes Mountains of Peru
  • Travels through 5 countries:
    • Bolivia
    • Brazil
    • Colombia
    • Ecuador
    • Peru
    • Venezuela
  • Flows into The Atlantic Ocean
